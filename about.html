My Journey

2020 - The Beginning

Started Salesforce journey as an Admin. First certification achieved, igniting a passion for the platform.

2021 - Expanding Skills

Earned Platform App Builder and Platform Developer I certifications. Started building custom solutions with Apex and Lightning.

2022 - Going Deep

Became a certified Salesforce Architect. Led enterprise implementations and discovered the power of OmniStudio.

2023 - AI Revolution

Dove into Einstein AI and Copilot. Built first AI-powered agents and realized the transformative potential of AI in Salesforce.

Started sharing knowledge through meetups and demos. Recognized as a Salesforce MVP for community contributions.

2025 - Pro Blazer Studio

Launched Pro Blazer Studio to create a dedicated space for builders to collaborate, innovate, and transform ideas into impact.
